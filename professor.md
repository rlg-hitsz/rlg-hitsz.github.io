Prof. Yanjie Li, associate professor of Harbin Institute of Technology, High-level talents in Shenzhen. He received his Ph.D. in engineering from the department of automation, University of Science and Technology of China, 2006. From 2006 to 2008, he did postdoctoral research in the department of electronics and computer engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. In 2008, he joined Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen) as a teacher. His research interests including Reinforcement Learning, Inverse Reinforcement Learning, Intelligent driving, Unmanned Intelligent Storage and UAV control. He got the award of the President of Chinese Academy of Sciences, Pan Qingyi Discrete Event System Execellent Thesis Award, etc. As the project leader, he has successively undertaken the national Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) youth fund project, ministry of education doctoral program fund project, national natural science foundation joint fund project, Shenzhen basic layout project and basic exploration project, etc. As a major participant, he has completed a number of national Natural Science Foundation projects and Hong Kong RGC Research fund projects, and published more than 60 academic papers, many of which have been published in international top journals such as Automatica, European Journal of Operational Research, IEEE Transactions on System Man and Cybernetics. He is a reviewer of IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control, Automatica, Discrete Event Dynamic Systems, IEEE Transactions on Cybernetics, International Journal of Control, IEEE Control and Decision Conference (CDC), ACC, IEEE ICIC, etc. Also, he is the member of the procedure committee of the China Control Conference (CCC). Now, he is a member of the Chinese association of automation, a member of the Chinese association of operational planning and an evaluation expert of the national Natural Science Foundation of China.
